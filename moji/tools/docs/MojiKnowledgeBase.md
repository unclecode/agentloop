## Privacy Settings

- **Profile Visibility**
  - **Public Profile**: If your profile is set to Public, your name, bio, ratings, following, and followers are visible to everyone. Public users can see your profile in search results and view your lists and activity. Liked and disliked movies are also visible to everyone.
  - **Private Profile**: Only your followers can view your profile information, including your posts, ratings, and lists. Users who do not follow you can only see basic details like your name and bio, but not your activity or full list of movies.
  - **Blocked Users**: Blocked users cannot find your profile in search or see any of your information, regardless of your profile's public or private setting.
  - **Managing Visibility**: To change your profile visibility, go to **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Profile Visibility** and choose between Public or Private.
- **Message Privacy**
  - **Private Profile**: Only your followers can send you messages.
  - **Public Profile**: Anyone can send you messages unless they are blocked.
  - **Blocking Users**: If you block someone, they will not be able to message you, even if your profile is public.
- **Follow Requests**
  - **Private Profile**: Others must send you a follow request, which you can accept or decline, to follow your account.
  - **Public Profile**: Anyone can follow you without approval. However, blocked users cannot send follow requests.
- **Managing Lists**
  - **Public Profile**: Your lists are visible to all Mojitofilms users. However, list-specific privacy settings may override profile settings.
  - **Private Profile**: Only your followers can see your lists.
  - **List-Specific Privacy**: Even if your profile is public, individual lists can be marked as private, restricting access to users who have been given explicit access.
- **List-Specific Privacy Settings**
  - **Private Lists**: Only users who have been granted access can view or edit the list. If a list is set to private, it will not be visible to the public or users who havenâ€™t been invited.
  - **Public Lists**: Public lists are visible to all Mojitofilms users, and they can be shared externally via links, the feed, or chat.
  - **List Role Access**: Different roles apply to lists, including **Owner**, **Editor**, and **Viewer**:
    - **Owner**: Can manage privacy settings, add collaborators, and control who can edit or view the list.
    - **Editor**: Can add and remove movies but cannot manage privacy or add collaborators.
    - **Viewer**: Can only view the list.
- **Sharing Lists**
  - **Public Lists**: Public lists can be shared via the Mojitofilms feed, chat, or an external link.
  - **Private Lists**: Cannot be shared publicly. They are restricted to invited users.
